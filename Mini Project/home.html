School Objectives

Kg-Kids offers a unique experience of early years education for 2-4 year olds. It is run in a beautiful thatched house surrounded extensive gardens, with a strong team of well qualified staff.

School

Kg-Kids aims to provide high quality care and education for children and a secure foundation for learning in an encouraging and nurturing environment where staff and parents or carers work in close partnership.

Unique Child

Development of personal independence, freedom to initiate activities and make choices Development of self-discipline and self-motivation.

Education

Provision of an inspiring environment in three different areas with access to different gardens where children can play freely and be involved in many different learning experiences.

Learning & Development

Enjoyment in learning through play and structured activities Development of imagination Opportunities for exploring literature and developing reading and writing skills Opportunities for mathematical development

Our Courses

Math Fundamentals

This course focusing on showing relating and using the whole numbers,describe about the shapes and spaces,know the numbers names and compare the numbers.

Science

This course includes the study about earth and space,learning about the gravity and its effect on objects.The students will learn to identify the sun,moon,stars and the differences between day and night.

Character Building

This course comprises of the thing that matters the most as education that are good habits which are necessary for being good human being.

Language Arts

Reading Skills

Counting & Printing
