About Our School

Welcome to KG-KIDS

Ideally, KG-KIDS is a smooth, sunny introduction to real school for your child, since it sets the stage for the rest of your child's education. While no program is perfect, some are better than others. Find out what sets them apart and how you can get the best possible start for your child — no matter what your options are, KG-KIDS provides all.

The development of self-esteem is one of the important goals of our school. This is the process of helping your child feel good about who he is and confident in his ability to tackle the challenges of learning.

KG-KIDS teaches cooperation, the ability to work, learn, and get along with others. A year in kindergarten provides your child with the opportunity to learn patience, as well as the ability to take turns, share, and listen to others

School Objectives

Kg-Kids offers a unique experience of early years education for 2-4 year olds. It is run in a beautiful thatched house surrounded extensive gardens, with a strong team of well qualified staff.

School

Kg-Kids aims to provide high quality care and education for children and a secure foundation for learning in an encouraging and nurturing environment where staff and parents or carers work in close partnership.

Unique Child

Development of personal independence, freedom to initiate activities and make choices Development of self-discipline and self-motivation.

Education

Provision of an inspiring environment in three different areas with access to different gardens where children can play freely and be involved in many different learning experiences.

Learning & Development

Enjoyment in learning through play and structured activities Development of imagination Opportunities for exploring literature and developing reading and writing skills Opportunities for mathematical development

Safeguard & Welfare

Keeping children safe in our care Ongoing staff training Implementation of health and safety policies

Communication

Freedom to express feelings, ideas and beliefs within a supportive environment Having fun, laughing, encouraging the development of humour Creation of a loving and caring environment in which each child's potential can be nurtured

All Round Development

Expand your child’s ability to learn about (and from) the world, organize information, and solve problems Provides a combination of formal (teacher-initiated) and informal (child-initiated) activities..
